The ECSL Story

The story of ECSL Steel Manufacturing and Trading Corporation is about Filipino ingenuity, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to customer service and quality workmanship.It all began with a small workshop in Navotas during the 1940s. The shop owner, Pablo Alcantara Santos, began as a humble builder of pedicab sidecars.
